Yeon-Ji Song

I am a third-year PhD student in Biointelligence Lab at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Byoung-Tak Zhang. Previously, I graduated from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) with a Bachelor's in Electronic and Computer Engineering.

During my undergrad, I worked on my Bachelor's Thesis on Autonomous Navigation and Object Detection with Reinforcement Learning in Robotics and Multi-Perception Lab advised by Prof. Ming Liu.

I am broadly interested in computer vision, representation learning, and reinforcement learning. Much of my work is based on the tools of deep learning, probabilistic learning, and methods that use domain-specific knowledge of geometry and graphics. I enjoy being inspired by neuroscience to understand human intelligence of AI.
